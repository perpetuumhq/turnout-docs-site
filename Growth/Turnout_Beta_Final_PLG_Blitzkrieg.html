1. Beta Launch Thesis

Why now, what we're proving, what success looks like. The bet: venue-through-content activations via mobile pages + WhatsApp concierge.

The Strategic Opportunity (Now)

India's go-out economy is shifting from posting-led behavior to presence-led behavior. Consumers increasingly want experiences that feel real, social, and shareable. Simultaneously, venue owners are trapped in aggregator dependency (Zomato takes 20-25% commission) with zero first-party consumer data or owned channels.

Turnout's wedge: venues want to shift from aggregator dependency to owned channels, and we can create that channel in 60 seconds with AI.

The Core Bet

Venue-Through-Content Activations: Instead of hunting consumers, hunt content creators. Creators share venue pages naturally because they get attributed bookings. Every page share becomes a referral loop. Every venue activation surfaces content proof that attracts other venue owners.

Mobile Pages + WhatsApp Concierge: Venues don't want dashboards. They want a landing page that their customers can access (no app install, no friction), and a WhatsApp companion that handles edge-case friction (group splits, price questions, timing issues, reassurance). Page converts intent; WhatsApp converts hesitation.

What Success Looks Like in 60 Days

Venue Metrics (By Day 60)

  • 50-75 venues onboarded across Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai
  • 45+ pages live (approval rate 90%+)
  • Time-to-live: <2 hours from onboarding to page in market
  • Page adoption: 80%+ of onboarded venues actively using pages
  • Theme representation: 3+ venues per theme (sports-social, brunch, wellness)

Consumer & Attribution Metrics (By Day 60)

  • 2,000+ unique page visits
  • 15%+ booking conversion (page view → WA handoff)
  • 200+ attributed bookings (page → reservation → show-up)
  • 5%+ secondary activation (invited friends become independent bookers)
  • 90%+ WA handoff-to-confirmation rate

Unit Economics Success Criterion

CAC (Cost to Acquire Venue): Rs 0 for first 50 (proof gathering). Growth CAC target: Rs 500-1,000 per venue by Wave 2.

LTV (Venue Lifetime Value): Rs 5,000-15,000 in Year 1 (campaign spend, attribution confidence, retention premium). Target 3:1 LTV:CAC by Month 3.

Learning Velocity: 3+ template variations tested per venue category. Success rate improvement 2-4% week-on-week through optimization.

The Validation Thesis

Beta is not about scale. Beta is about proving three things:

  1. Venue pain is real: Venues genuinely want owned channels and are willing to activate if setup is <5 minutes.
  2. Pages work: AI-generated pages from venue IG + website data drive measurable bookings with attribution accuracy ≥80%.
  3. Loops compound: First 10 venues → proof → 20 more venues via word-of-mouth (K-factor ≥0.4). Content creators share pages, creating organic acquisition flywheel.

4. PLG Growth Engine: The 4 Patterns That Compound

How Turnout acquires venues and consumers through pure product mechanics.

The Four PLG Patterns

1. Output IS the Ad

Mechanic: Venue page is not a listing; it's a shareable artifact. When owner shares page link in their IG story or sends to a friend, the link becomes a referral. Every share = organic acquisition funnel.

Proof: If 10 venues go live and each gets 50 shares (organic), that's 500 discovery events with zero paid spend.

2. Instant Output

Mechanic: Venue sees their page live in <2 minutes after onboarding. This creates instant validation. Owner shows page to 2-3 staff members, they share internally. Viral loop inside venue.

Proof: Speed = credibility. Slower product → venue owner waits days → loses excitement → cancels.

3. Free Hook → Paid Upsell

Mechanic: Free tier: page generation + 10 WA conversations/month. Growth tier: campaign management + full concierge + attribution reports + multi-page templates.

Proof: First 50 venues free (proof gathering). Wave 2 venues: pricing introduced. Expected free-to-paid conversion: 10-15% (vs 3-5% industry baseline) due to attribution proof.

4. Embedded Virality

Mechanic: Every share card, invite link, split-pay link, creator attribution link = referral event. Consumer shares booking → friend clicks → discovers page → friend becomes secondary activation.

Proof: Target: 5-10% of first-time bookers refer 1 friend. Viral coefficient k ≥0.05-0.1.

Growth Loops Detailed

10. Risk Register & Mitigation

What can go wrong and how we prevent it.

Risk 1: Qualification Engine Doesn't Filter Properly

Impact: Onboard low-quality venues (bad photos, no pricing, low engagement). They activate but produce zero bookings. Waste time, no proof.

Mitigation: Validate threshold (≥55 for launch) on first 5-10 venue applications. If threshold wrong, recalibrate before mass onboarding.

Trigger: If ≥30% of venues produce <1 page visit in first 3 days, pause and recalibrate.

Risk 2: Creator Partnerships Don't Convert

Impact: Creators share Turnout pages but viewers don't click. Creator reach is wasted.

Mitigation: Test with 3-5 creators first. Measure Reel CTR on first 3 collaborations. If <3%, revise messaging/CTA before scaling to 15+.

Trigger: Pause creator outreach if first 5 Reels average <5% CTR. Diagnosis phase: test different CTAs, landing page copy.

Risk 3: WA Concierge Bottleneck

Impact: WA inquiries spike but concierge response time increases to >2 hrs. Conversion drops. Venue owners get frustrated.

Mitigation: Set up chatbot escalation (80% of questions answerable by template bot; 20% routed to human). Daily concierge schedule: 10am-6pm coverage.

Trigger: If >20% of WA threads have >2hr first-response time, activate second concierge operator.

Risk 4: No-Show Rate >40%

Impact: Bookings confirm but customers don't show up. Venue owner sees: "5 bookings, 2 show-ups." Trust in platform breaks.

Mitigation: Add reminder + incentive layer. 24h before booking: auto-send WA reminder + offer (e.g., free drink). Test on first 20 bookings.

Trigger: If show-up rate <60% by Day 14, activate reminder + incentive.

Risk 5: Attribution Confidence <70%

Impact: Venue owner sees bookings but doubts attribution. Can't trace which page/creator/share caused which booking. Loses proof value.

Mitigation: Implement multi-layer tracking: UTM params on all links, WA source tracking, survey at concierge (ask: "How did you find us?"), page analytics.

Trigger: If reconciliation between page analytics + booking source shows >30% discrepancy by Day 7, investigate tracking implementation.

Risk 6: Venue Owner Activation Friction

Impact: Onboarding takes >2 hours instead of <60 mins. Owner gives up. Page never goes live.

Mitigation: Async approval flow by Day 3. Owner approves via 1-min Loom video + auto-generate page from IG + website (no manual input).

Trigger: If >20% of venues haven't approved pages by Day 2 after onboarding, shift to async flow.

Strategic Guardrails

Do Not Ship If:

  • Page generation fails on ≥30% of venue IG handles (technical risk)
  • Attribution accuracy <60% by Day 14 (proof risk)
  • Venue approval rate <70% on first variant (UX risk)
  • WA infrastructure fails for >1 hour (concierge downtime)

Pause Expansion If:

  • K-factor drops below 0.2 (referral loop broken)
  • Venue retention <75% after 7 days (product doesn't hold)
  • No-show rate >50% (proof confidence breaks)
  • Free-to-paid conversion <5% by Day 60 (monetization signal weak)
